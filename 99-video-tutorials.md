## How to use these videos

1. **First pass - John Savill AZ-305 Study Cram** (one sitting). Use the latest version on his channel - he refreshes it whenever Microsoft updates the skills measured. It surfaces every design tradeoff the exam can ask about.
2. **Second pass - Savill's AZ-305 Master Class** for any tradeoff area where you didn't feel confident (identity, data platform, business continuity, infrastructure).
3. **Third pass - Azure Friday + WAF series** for the *why* behind each design choice. AZ-305 is design-and-tradeoff heavy; understanding **why** the best answer is best (cost vs. performance vs. resilience vs. operations) is what earns the points.
4. Then take the **Practice Assessment** (see that page in the sidebar) before scheduling the real exam.

> Tip: AZ-305 questions almost always include 2-3 *technically correct* options. Savill's framing of "what is the *best fit* given the constraints in the scenario" is exactly the framing the exam uses.
